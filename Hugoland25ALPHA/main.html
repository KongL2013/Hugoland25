Encyclopedia of Hugoland

History

Hugoland is a **fantasy country** with two states: **Chipp** and **Skeld**. It has a balance of **peaceful daily life** and **deep mysteries** for those who seek adventure.

Notable Figures:
Dr. Hugo Ismail – The **founder of Hugoland**.
All fallen soldiers from the 2013 war are considered legendary.

Geography & Landmarks

Chipp: Home to the **Castle of Chipp**, which has a fireplace that has burned **mysteriously for 500 years** without fuel.

Skeld: Features **The Big 4**, where all four seasons meet over four months.

Other landmarks include the **Skeld Mountains, Holiday Fiesta, and Never-Ending Trail**.

Hugoland experiences an annual **Candy Storm**, where candy falls from the sky with no trace.

Society & Government

Hugoland is a **democracy**, with a **beloved, powerful, and trusted king** as the highest authority.

The **Hugoland Magic Authority (HMA)** regulates magic, which requires a **special license** to use.

The HMA was **corrupt from 2014-2020** but has since been restored.

Magic

The use of magic in Hugoland is regulated by the **Hugoland Magic Authority (HMA)**. To use magic, individuals need to apply for a **Magic License**.

Economy & Lifestyle

The currency is the **Hugoland Dollar (HLD)**. People enjoy a **high quality of life** with **minimal poverty, less school/homework, and shorter work hours**.

Society is built on **technology (50% more advanced than the real world)** and **agriculture**.

Culture & Events

Hugoland hosts **the Hugolympics**, a major sporting event at the **Grand Stadium in Chipp**. Hugoland has the **most and best athletes**, with **over 5,000 athletes** in the finals and **3,200+ medals/trophies** won.

Celebrates unique holidays like **National Ice Cream Day, National Exercise Week, National Green Month**, all known holidays, and surprise holidays.

Mysteries & Conflict

**The Mystery of 2207**: A series of **9 puzzles** that led **80 people into oblivion**—no bodies, no trace, no confirmed deaths, and no one knows if they are still alive.

Hugoland has a **2% chance of sinking by 2030**—if it survives, it will never sink.

Hugoland fought two wars: **2013 War for Independence** and **2020 War to join the TAG group**.

The country's **golden age was in 2023**, with **peak territory, the strongest currency, and <1% poverty**.

The Mystery of 2207

The mystery is still actively and cautiously investigated. The message left behind is: **122,71,87,88**.
